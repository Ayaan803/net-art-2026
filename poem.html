In Your Eyes

This piece is inspired by the song In Your Eyes, which explores the idea of seeing the truth in someones expression even when their words say otherwise. At night, emotions feel louder, and love feels more fragile  especially when honesty is missing.


The story reflects emotional distance and denial. Someone can smile, laugh, and stay close, yet their eyes reveal something deeper. What hurts most is not the ending, but the pretending that everything is fine when it clearly is not. The eyes become a mirror, showing fear, regret, and unspoken truth.

Major Themes

  • Emotional denial
  • Unspoken truth
  • Love and vulnerability

What the Eyes Reveal

  1. Hidden pain
  2. Fear of honesty
  3. Desire to be understood

The song encourages listeners to pay attention to what people show, not just what they say. You can learn more about the artist and his work at The Weeknds official website.
